Privacy-Statement

Hey neighbors!

This is a community bulletin board for our building, meant to help us share updates, events, and important info.

What We Collect: We only collect the info you choose to share: posts, and contact details you provide. No tracking, no ads.

How We Use It: Your info stays here, within our building's private system. It's only used for communication between residents.

Who Can See It: Other USERS can see posts.

Security: This board is self-hosted and not shared with third parties. However, please be mindful of what you post, as nothing online is ever 100% private.

Questions? If you have concerns or suggestions, just reach out to me!

Let's keep this space friendly and useful!
